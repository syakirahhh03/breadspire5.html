About BreadSpire

Know more about us!


OUR VISION

"At BreadSpire Bakery, our vision is to bring joy with every bite. We create delicious, handcrafted treats that inspire happiness and connect our community. Through quality ingredients and creative baking, we aim to be the place where smiles are shared and memories are made."

OUR MISSION

"To create delicious, high-quality treats that bring joy to our customers, using fresh ingredients and a passion for baking."

OUR OBJECTIVES

    1. Create Delicious Treats
    2. Use Quality Ingredients
    3. Create Customer Loyalty
    4. Support Sustainability
    5. Expand Offerings

OUR HISTORY

Founded in 2022 by Nurul Aina Fathihah binti Ahmad Fuad, BreadSpire Bakery started as a small home-based business with a dream to share delicious homemade baked goods. Over time, it grew into a beloved local bakery known for its warmth, quality, and creativity in baking.
